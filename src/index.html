About me

Hello! My name is Bruno Correia, I am 22 years old and I am from Jundiaí, São Paulo. I have been working with technology since I was 13 years old, starting at a friend's electronics repair shop. Although my dream was to become a professional E-Sports player—a career in which I joined an academy team—I eventually transitioned to the programming field, which has always fascinated me more. Today, I am a dedicated professional, constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow in the technology sector.

In my free time, I enjoy playing football and online games, activities that allow me to relax and have fun. I also love exploring new cafes and bars, enjoying different environments and flavors. These hobbies help me balance my professional and personal life, providing moments of relaxation and socialization.
